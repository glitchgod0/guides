* ![A green square with a dashed outline.](/assets/rb3pc/config/smallgreen.png "Green Square") **REQUIRED**: 
	* **Change `Network Status` to `Connected`. - If left on `Disconnected`, the game will temporarily freeze when browsing the song library.**

* ![A tan square with a solid outline.](/assets/rb3pc/config/smalltan.png "Tan Square") **For online multiplayer**: 
	* To play Rock Band 3 online, add Rock Band Enhanced's Server IP.
		* Set `IP/Hosts switches` to `rb3ps3live.hmxservices.com=45.33.44.103`.
		* Either tick **`Enable UPNP`** or **forward port 9103 (UDP) in your firewall**. - This is necessary to play with more than 2 players.
